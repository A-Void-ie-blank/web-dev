Scissor Seven

Scissor Seven (also known as Killer 7) is a Chinese donghua series. It was released internationally under the name Scissor Seven on Netflix. A film sequel to the series' third season was announced at the end of the season's final episode.The fourth season premiered on January 18, 2023.
